Quill - Privacy Policy

As with many websites, we collect the e-mail addresses of those who communicate with us via e-mail, aggregate information on what pages consumers access or visit, and information volunteered by the user. This information is used to improve the app and never shared with or sold to other organizations for commercial purposes, except to provide products or services you've requested, when we have your permission, or if it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of our Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law.

Crash logs

We send anonymized information about app crashes to a third-party server. This anonymized information is only ever used for analyzing and fixing bugs in the app.

Questions

Any questions about this privacy policy should be addressed to the developer of the app, whose contact details can be found in the app's Play Store listing.
